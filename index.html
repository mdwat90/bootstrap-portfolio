About Me

Hello! My name is Michael David Watson, but I traditionally go by David. I am originally from Athens, AL, but moved to Colorado with my wife from Atlanta, GA in January, 2018. I am a part-time preacher at the church I attend. When not working, you can find me either hanging out with my wife at home or outdoors mountain biking, playing soccer, or being physically active in some way.

My passion for coding developed over the course of the last year as I began my journey toward web development through UX Design. I took an online course at General Assembly, and since then, I have built my own portfolio and developed a passion for writing code. I took another online course through Udemy to learn the basics of HTML and CSS, as well as a little JavaScript. Now, I am fully committed to the development of my skills by completing the University of Denver's Coding Bootcamp and creating a new path for myself in the web development world.
